Rules and Important Game Info

The way Weeble works is not entirely comparable to Wordle and other variants.

Guessable word-based categories will show the exact answer, but number-based categories will only be shown down to a certain threshold, indicated by the yellow symbol.

Each incorrect guess will reveal one more tag (in order of relevancy) and genre of the correct anime. Don't worry–tags marked as spoilers on AniList will NOT be shown.

Even though tags and genres will show up, they are not included in the filter and cannot be guessed.

Cookies and Privacy

User information is never sent from Weeble to any server. All information except for the daily anime is stored client side. Various info for weeble is stored in a mix of cookies and local storage, so if you clear your cookies or storage, expect for Weeble to forget about you and everyone you've ever loved as well as your daily guess data.
